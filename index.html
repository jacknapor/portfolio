About Me

My name is Jack Napor, and I'm currently a computer science student at Bucknell University. I am finishing my senior year, and expect to graduate this May with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

My passion has always been for video games- both playing them and developing them. They have been present in my life since I was a young child, and their influence on me can not be understated. The unique ability video games held as an artform to fully captivate and immerse its players in a interactive, living, breathing world was quickly evident to me. My experiences exploring these worlds served as my inspiration to focus my education around studying computer science. My academic pursuit of computer science began in high school, and continued through my education at Bucknell, all due to the beauty,wonder,comradery, and personal growth I experienced while immersed in these virtual worlds. My dream is to create my own worlds and tell stories through video games, in hopes that one day others can experience those same kinds of powerful, meaningful experiences that invoked the very passion I have built my life around.
