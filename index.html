It is to strive every day to further humanity's understanding of the universe's seemingly infinite number of astounding complexities, and to use that knowledge to foster innovation and invention. It is through the creativity and undying passion of engineers that technologies capable of catalyzing the development of our species and globally improving quality of life are born. Zoms

Introduction

As part of our Software Engineering and Design course, three other students and I created "Zoms", a 2D zombie survival game made in Java and inspired by the "Zombies" game mode in the Call of Duty series. After completing the course, I spent a significant amount of time polishing the UI, fixing bugs, and adding gameplay features. Features I added on my own include sound effects, music, and the damage animation.

Key Features

  • Survive waves of increasing amounts of zombies that gain speed every round.
  • Collect temporary upgrades that spawn around the map.
  • Earn points killing zombies and exchange them at the shop for ammo, health, armor or permanent upgrades.
  • Includes 3 different maps.
  • Try to reach the highest round you can before being overwhelmed.

View Source Code

A playable version of my game along with its source code is available for download on GitHub at: https://github.com/jrn011/Zoms

VisiTour

Introduction

VisiTour is an android application developed to make it easier for users around the world to organize their travel plans and keep track of places they've visited. It was developed as part of my Senior Design course, and the idea was inspired by the client assigned to us. He wanted an easy, user friendly way to keep track of which national parks he had visited and which he hadn't, and wanted it to be accessible on his mobile device to ensure easy access during his travels.

Key Features

  • Easily navigatable world map.
  • Search feature integrated with Google Maps API for easy location finding.
  • User list creation to organize travel locations.
  • Mark locations as visited and track progress towards visiting all locations in a list.
  • Displays any location in a list on the map.
  • Provides directions to any location in a list seamlessly with Google Maps.
  • Facebook Integration (Login/Sharing).

Download Now

The application is free and publicly available for Android devices on the Google Play store.

TripleHax

Introduction

During high school, I wanted a way to start combining my passion for gaming with my growing computer science knowledge. I decided to found a company, TripleHax LLC, and develop a website originally located at www.triplehax.com. I updated the site with reviews for video games I had played and with relevant news in the gaming industry. The website and company were recently dissolved, however the community generated about 500 likes on facebook and had 200 site members while it was active.

Key Features

  • User account creation & login.
  • Article search feature.
  • User comments on articles.
  • Facebook like/share capabilites for articles & website.
  • User access to an embedded arcade.
  • Integration with Google AdSense to support placement of ads throughout the site.
